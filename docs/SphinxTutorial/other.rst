Usage with other way
======================

In reality,  learning a new method or language is hard. So, there are many proper methods for making 'sphinx' pages using another way.

In this section, we will learn how to take advantage of prior knowledge. Such as Jupyter notebook and Markdown. It cannot be the best solution for the documentation but better. Cause of the basic syntax of 'sphinx', you cannot be free at all from rst(reSturcturedText) but almost be.

The main topic of this section is the usage of rst syntax, especially 'Directive', into Jupyter notebook and Markdown.
